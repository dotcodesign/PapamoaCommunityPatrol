Papamoa Community Patrol

Welcome to Papamoa Community Patrol


The Papamoa Community Patrol Inc. PCP is a voluntary group of citizens giving some of their time to assist the Police to make the Papamoa community a safer environment in which to live. Current membership is about 50. Our Patrol is affiliated to the national umbrella organisation, Community Patrols of New Zealand, which operates under a formal agreement with the NZ Police.

Community Patrollers act as 'eyes and ears' for the Police. Operating in organised pairs in a fully equipped, sign-written Patrol Vehicle, patrollers note any activity which is unusual or looks suspicious (e.g. late- night activity on a building site).

Find out more about Community Patrols New Zealand here

    What we do:
  • Three times a day, Day, Dusk and Night, our dedicated volunteers patrol the streets of Papamoa in car and on foot
  • We meet regularly with Papamoa Police for upto date taskings and information
  • Actively train in first aid and civil defence response, allowing us to be ready for anything
  • We run 'Safer Plates Days', securing number plates with tamper proof screws

Volunteer with us
